Our Story

Founded in 2018, Oswal Gift and Stationary began with a simple belief: that beautiful stationery has the power to transform ordinary moments into extraordinary experiences. We source the finest materials and work with skilled artisans to create products that inspire creativity and bring joy to everyday writing.

Our commitment to quality extends beyond our products. We believe in sustainable practices, supporting local artisans, and creating a community of writers, artists, and creators who share our passion for the written word.
